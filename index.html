About Me

My name is Chris Mitchell. Currently, I am the Assistant Store Manager at the Seward Co-op Friendship Store. There I work to ensure staff and customer safety, maintenance of the physical store structure, and support of sales departments. As for personnel, I manage the facilities department's two roles, Manager on Duty and Housekeeper. I am responsible for directing the goals for the department, ensure proper training, develop new practices, ensure efficient work flow, and hold staff accountable to the department's and organization's policies and expectations.

While my experience as the Assistant Store Manager has provided me with a tremendous amount of important lessons and growth, I have decided to begin my first steps toward a career that is within my interests. Coding Bootcamp is a program that I am currently participating in to help teach me fundamental full stack web development. I believe that this program will give me the tools to help push me into a field that I find both exciting and rewarding.
